Floral Care Tips

Keep your flowers fresh and blooming with these expert care tips.

General Flower Care Tips

Follow these essential care tips for all types of flowers:

  • Change the water every 2-3 days to keep it fresh.
  • Trim the stems at an angle before placing them in the vase.
  • Remove any leaves that will be submerged in the water to avoid bacteria.
  • Keep flowers out of direct sunlight and away from heating vents.

Caring for Specific Types of Flowers

Roses

Roses need plenty of fresh water and daily trimming of the stems to remain hydrated.

Lilies

Lilies prefer cooler environments and require fresh water every day to stay healthy.

Tulips

Tulips are sensitive to light and should be kept in a cool area with partial light exposure.
